MY LOBBY

HOW IT WORKS

1.Enable the chat bot (below), then tell your chat: type !play to join the race, !boost to speed their ball up.
2.Add the OBS link as a Browser Source so the race shows on your stream.
3.Turn on AUTOPLAY (or hit START RACE) — races run on stream automatically.
★The PLAY link is optional — share it only if a viewer wants their own first-person view.
